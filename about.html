Our Roadmap

While we're only just getting started, we've already had quite the history! Through each twist and turn, it's been the people who have made our journey so memorable.

January, 2022

Georgetown Blockchain Created

Thanks to an initial $10,000 seed contribution from our founder, Georgetown Blockchain was able to induct its inaugural class of analysts.

May, 2022

First Successful Semester

Thanks to the hard work of its contributors, Georgetown Blockchain grows its capital, industry connections, and operations.

August, 2022

Released Whitepaper

Over the summer, contributors worked hard to finalize and release the clubs whitepaper, formalizing operations, voting delegations, etc.

Raise Funding

By December, 2022, we will have raised funding from partnering organizations, allowing us fulfill our mission more effectively.

Receive Governance Delegation

By the end of the Spring 2023 semester, Georgetown Blockchain hopes to hurdle the legal obstacles and form D.C.'s first DAO among university students.
