What do we look for?

Commitment

We're looking for people who have shown how they're willing to put in the time to understand new and confusing concepts. Additionally, we want to see that, whatever you have done in the past, you have put your heart and soul into doing the best job you can.

Forward-thinking

The blockchain industry is rapidly changing, evolving, and innovating, so we're looking for people who know how to keep up with such a dynamic technology. We want to see that, in whatever you've done before, you've had a vision for a better future and you acted on making it a reality in a calm and calculated manner.

Leadership

Georgetown Blockchain is a community of leaders. Because our organization has so many sectors, so many roles in which trust is placed, we need people who can lead projects, thinking, and other members. We also need people who are willing to venture out, discover and bring a new idea or operation to the organization.

Application Process

Written Application

Your written application will provide us the opportunity to get to know you, your strengths, and your passions better. We don't expect you to be a blockchain guru, just show us what you know and what you want to learn!

The Interviews

The interviews are our way of getting to know you in a personal way. You can expect questions including those involving what we look for in an applicant, your experience and understanding of the blockchain space, and more.
